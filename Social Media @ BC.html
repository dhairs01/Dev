    Social Media @ BC


     

    At Baker College, we have social media accounts across several platforms to showcase the student experience on our campuses, to present calendars of events taking place across the system, and to provide a platform for connection and engagement with students, parents, faculty, staff, alumni and community members.  We encourage you to follow along and engage with the Baker College community by submitting your group's or program's experiences to our pages through our Social Media and/or Event Requests portal.


    We ask that you complete the Social Media Request Form 4-5 weeks before the scheduled event, allowing adequate time to properly promote and market the event(s) on our social media pages and our website's Event Calendar.  Our process will be to review the request and, when needed, send a follow-up email with questions to help clarify the request.

    Don't forget to visit our social media pages and to like, comment and share our posts to further their reach!

    If you have any questions, please email us at getsocial@baker.edu.
