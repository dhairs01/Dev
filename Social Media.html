    At Baker College, we have social media accounts across several platforms to showcase the student experience on our campuses, to present calendars of events taking place across the system, and to provide a platform for connection and engagement with students, parents, faculty, staff, alumni and community members. We encourage you to follow along and engage with the Baker College community by submitting your group's or program's experiences to our pages through our Social Media and/or Event Requests portal.
